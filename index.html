About James

James is an aspiring software engineer based in Plymouth, England. James currently studies BSc(Hons) Computing at the University of Plymouth.

James is originally from Plymouth, England with family from the United States of America. As well as studying for his degree in Computing, James is also the Chairman & Co-Founder of the Plymouth University Computer/Computing Society. James also volunteers weekly at the Plymouth Central Library to help out with 'Hello World', a coding club aimed at young children. This is because James recognises the importance of introducing code and technology to a younger audience.

James has been programming incessantly over the last six years, often making websites, apps or remaking games that he played during his childhood.

James currently shows significant interest in desktop application development & native Android development.

C# Desktop

James shows particular interest in desktop application development, his language of choice is C#. James has become very familiar with this robust, multi-purpose programming language during the 2 years he has been using it.
