About Me

Software Engineer

I am a software engineer currently based in Plymouth where I am in the final stage of my Computer Science degree. I have had a professional career in software engineering for the past 3 years, taking me all around the country, most notably Cheltenham, where I spent a year working for GE (General Electric).

I have experience in several different areas of software engineering including but not limited to: software testing, object oriented programming, web design & development, API & database development, CI/CD (Jenkins), Git, Agile software methodologies, mobile (app development) and desktop development. Alongside the above, I am familiar with several software development architectures and have hands-on experience with MVC & MVVM.

Over my time in the software industry, I have accrued years of experience with the following technologies: .NET Framework (C#, WPF, Windows Forms, Xamarin), PHP, Java, C, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, SQL (SQL Server), PLSQL (Oracle SQL), Jenkins, Python and version control with Git (GitHub & BitBucket).

Outside of the office, I enjoy travelling to different parts of the world (often visiting my family in the USA), amateur photography and staying healthy by taking part in regular physical exercise.
